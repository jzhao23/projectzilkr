STEP 3: MARKET SEGMENTATION

Here’s where you brainstorm all the possible uses of your idea, even the crazy stuff. Think of all the problems your solution can solve and write them all down. Then you can start narrowing down to the market that suits you best based on opportunity, possible share and accessibility. This market is called your Beachhead market, and it’s what you’ll keep in mind when you design your product.

Isolating the goal.

Take some time to think about your passions and common problems you wish you could address. Maybe you want to enable youth worldwide in the realm of entrepreneurship, or connect them with resources that will help level the playing field. Try to develop a clear and detailed idea of what this goal is and why you want to achieve it. Whatever your drive may be, the most important thing to remember is that everything is possible.

Brainstorming solutions.

Now that you have a clarified concept, it's time to develop your game plan. This part of the process is often facilitated with research. Try to find companies or organizations that have walked the path before you. Analyze why they were successful or not so successful and use their experience to your benefit. Can't find anything? Don't worry, being a pioneer isn't a bad thing. In fact, it sets you apart and can only add excitement to your adventure!
